How we charge

Flat monthly retainer. No surprises.

We charge a flat monthly fee — not a percentage of your ad spend. That keeps our incentives aligned with your profitability, not with pushing you to spend more.

01

A predictable retainer billed on the 1st of each month. No commission on ad spend, no hidden percentages.

Common questions

Pricing FAQ

Is there a setup fee?

No. The first month's retainer covers all onboarding, audit, tracking install, and account build work.

How long is the contract?

We work month-to-month with a 30-day cancellation notice. We've found that performance-based partnerships work better than long lock-ins.

Do you take a percentage of ad spend?

No. We're a flat-fee shop. Percentage-of-spend pricing creates the wrong incentives — it rewards spending more, not spending well.

What if my ad spend grows past my tier?

We'll let you know in advance and move you up to the next tier the following month. No retroactive billing, ever.

Do you offer one-time projects?

Occasionally — typically for account audits or one-off launch builds. Reach out and we'll see if it's a fit.

Ready to find your tier?

Book a free consultation. We'll review your account and recommend the right starting point — no pressure either way.
